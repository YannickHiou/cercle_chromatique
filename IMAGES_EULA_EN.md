The images are produced using a color table and three parameters Rt, Rs and Rp, which are the radii of concentric circles separating the areas of tertiary, secondary and primary colors.  
The original configuration sets Rt to 200, and calculates Rs and Rp so that the surface area of tertiary colors is three times that of primary colors, and the surface area of alternating secondary colors is twice that of primary colors, which contributes to a certain balance in the design.
Any other color table and combination 0 < Rp < Rs < Rt constitute an acceptable parameterization and allow the program to generate the images covered by this EULA.
